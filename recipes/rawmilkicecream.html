Raw Milk Ice Cream

This delicious and nutritious ice cream recipe will change the way you think about health. Made with whole- nutrient dense ingredients and simple to make, you can enjoy this for dessert, breakfast or just a snack!

Tools and Ingredients

  • Blender
  • Ice cream maker
  • 1.5 cups whole raw milk
  • 2 cups raw cream
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or honey

Steps

  1. Place ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into an ice cream maker and follow instructions according to the ice cream maker directions. Usually this takes about 15-25 minutes.
  3. Serve or store in a container with a lid, and place in the freezer.
